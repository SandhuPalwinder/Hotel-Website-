About Us

Active Tour And Travels has been serving the community with honest and reliable service. Because of that honesty and reliability our customer base has grown far beyond the borders of the GTA.
When you contact our agency for travel information, you will encounter friendly, competent travel professionals who will provide you with current and convenient schedules in addition to the lowest available fares.
Our travel agency specializes in India for leisure travellers, offering an intimate knowledge of the country and services available to travellers. We offer costumized to tours of India ranging from 4 days to 35 days. If you are tired of the same yearly trip to Caribbean or Europe and you are looking for a more exotic vacation, India is a must for you.
Our unique chauffeured tours will never be a bore as you can travel and see all India has to offer at your own pace. We can also arrange groups from 8 to 40 people. With tours by Active Travels you will enjoy your private tour in all 5 star luxury hotels for less than you would pay for a bus tour. Our tours have guaranteed departures because all you need is your travel companion. These are a few of the types of tours we customize; cultural tours, special interest tours, Gurdwara tours, buddist tours, hindu temple tours, water rafting tours, kerala tours, hertitage tours, adventure tours, ayurvedic packages, yoga and meditation tours, palace on wheels, desert tours, wildlife and gaming tours. India has something for everyone.
Getting married and don't need six cappucino machines? How about a great honeymoon as an alternative!! Register a honeymoon and invite your friends and family to contribute to the vacation of your dreams.
Relatives coming from India? Relatives coming for a wedding or just a short visit or have they become new immigrants. We will send airline tickets right to their home. As soon as your booking is done with Active Travels your relatives will have their tickets in their hand within just 3 days.
